Wemo Explorer

About Wemo

Wemo is a survial/exploration game. Its unique features are uncovering clouds and having to survive the night. I built most of it in fall 2017, and have added a few things since then.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move the man around and uncover the world.

Use the following keys to do specific actions:

  • B: open the build menu
  • C: chop down a tree
  • D: drop an item from your backpack
  • E: eat a berry from a berry tree or your basket
  • F: feed a fire or add to a construction site
  • G: pick up an item or pick berries into your basket
  • J: jump onto or off of your raft
  • K: open the cook menu
  • S: go to sleep or wake up
  • X: center the screen on the man
